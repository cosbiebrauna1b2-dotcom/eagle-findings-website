Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our property sourcing and investment process

What types of properties do you source?

We specialize in sourcing a wide range of residential and commercial properties, including buy-to-let, HMOs, multi-unit blocks, and development sites. Our focus is on high-yield opportunities in the Southeast UK & Midlands.

How do you find off-market properties?

We leverage our extensive network of estate agents, mortgage brokers, and property professionals, along with our advanced technology, to identify properties before they are publicly listed. This gives our clients a competitive edge.

What is the typical timeframe for sourcing a property?

The timeframe can vary depending on your specific criteria and market conditions. However, our streamlined process and exclusive access to opportunities allow us to move quickly to secure deals for you.

Do you work with new investors?

Absolutely. We are passionate about helping first-time investors build a solid foundation. Our end-to-end support ensures you have all the guidance you need from your initial consultation to property completion.
